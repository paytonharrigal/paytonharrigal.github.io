Frequently Asked Questions

    -How much are dues? -> For the Semester it is $60 and for the Year it is $120.

    -Do I have to attend every practice? -> Nope just come out to the practices that you want to!

    -Do I need a bike to join the Club? -> No you do not need a bike to join the club however if you do want to do any of the bike workouts or races it is recommended that you have a bike. The Club can help you with your search for a bike with some useful resources such as facebook marketplace and bikesdirect.

    -Do we cover race fees? -> No we do not cover race fees however we will help provide transportation/ fuel reimbursements and a place to stay if the race is further away.

    -What if I can't swim? -> That's fine our Wednesday and Sunday swims would be great to come to. We have plenty of experienced swimmers on the team that can help teach you the proper for and provide you with resources to help you learn and improve at swimming.

    -Does the club throw parties? -> Yes we do have club gatherings.

    If you have any other questions that have not yet been answered then feel free to email us or DM us on Instagram you can find those links in the Contact Us Tab.
